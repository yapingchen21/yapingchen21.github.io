陳雅苹

Cognitive Neuroscientist & Occupational Therapist

I am currently a Postdoc, working with Dr. Nathan Weisz in the Salzburg Brain Dynamics lab. My primary research question is how human beings successfully process degraded speech.
I also have interests in a couple of topics in cognitive neuroscience. I have explored tinnitus with Dr. Nadia Müller–Voggel at the TinnLab. I have delved into healthy ageing with Dr. Chih-Mao Huang and Dr. Hsu-Wen Huang, vision rehabilitation with Dr. Li-Ting Tsai, and visual working memory with Dr. Bo-Cheng Kuo.
